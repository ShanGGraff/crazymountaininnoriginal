The last best place.. with a little something for everyone!

 

urrounded by some of the most beautiful land in the world, Martinsdale is perfect for anyone who enjoys the outdoors. It is a short drive from both the Bonanza and Martinsdale Reservoirs and there are several other Reservoirs and lakes in the area. There is also great creek fishing and the Musselshell River is right near by. Because it is just a few miles from forest service in the Crazies and Castle mountains the area is perfect for spring and summer outdoor activities such as:

Amazing hikes, world class fishing, horseback riding, ghost town exploration, and visiting any of the numerous historical sites and Museums..

 

... nd things aren't any less exciting during the winter and fall months. An extremely popular destination for both locals and visitors, the Crazy Mountain Inn caters to fisherman and hunters year around. To top things off, Martinsdale is just a short drive from the Showdown (down hill), Silvercrest (cross country), and King's Hill winter recreation areas. While the Inn provides great lodging at a great price the restaurant serves the best food for miles making it the perfect stop for anybody, anytime. Popular winter activities include:

Hunting, ice fishing, snowmobiling, and skiing.
